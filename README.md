---

## 🚨 What is Phishing?
Phishing is a cyberattack where attackers trick users into giving sensitive information like passwords, credit card details, or personal data.

---

## ⚠️ Types of Phishing
- Email Phishing  
- Spear Phishing  
- Vishing (Voice Phishing)  
- Smishing (SMS Phishing)  
- Social Media Phishing  
- Website Forgery  

---

## 🛡️ Prevention Tips
- Do not click suspicious links  
- Verify email sender  
- Avoid downloading unknown attachments  
- Use secure websites (HTTPS)  
- Never share passwords or OTP
